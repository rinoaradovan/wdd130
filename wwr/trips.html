ONE-DAY TRIP

This trip is a 4-5 hour adventure that covers 18-38km of rapids like "Huacas" and "Rodeo," with wildlife spotting and a riverside lunch, expect early starts around 5-6 AM from San Jose and return by evening. Safety briefings, helmets, life jackets, and bilingual guides are standard, often with short hikes to waterfalls. Prices start around $125, ideal for day-trippers seeking thrills amid toucans and monkeys.

TWO-DAYS TRIP

Overnight packages combine rafting with rainforest lodge or tent stays, featuring two days of paddling plus full board meals. You'll raft through canyons, enjoy eco-lodge dinners, and wake to jungle sounds before final rapids like "Dos MontaÃ±as." Perfect for immersion, including transfers and equipment.

THREE-DAYS TRIP

Extended expeditions raft, 14-18 miles daily via Class 3-4 rapids, with 2 nights at ecolodges, 3 breakfasts, 3 lunches, 2 dinners, and safety kayakers. Day 1: Short paddle to lodge; Day 2: Full adventure; Day 3: Major rapids and picnic lunch before San Jose return. Suited for active travelers wanting deeper nature exploration.

FOUR-DAYS TRIP

This epic expedition trip includes transportation from San Jose hotels, all safety gears, bilingual expert guides, and safety kayakers. Meals include daily breakfasts, lunches, and dinners. Lodging is 3-4 nights at riverside ecolodges with comfy beds, hot showers, and views. Multi-adventure versions add canyoneering, ziplining, Tarzan swing, and waterfall swims over 4 nights at lodges like Turrialba Vista/Rio Vista. Perfect for ultimate adrenaline and scenery.
